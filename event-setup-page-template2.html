Design by Ding3000

Beater is a whisk that is sculptural, space-saving and functional all at the same time. Beater can be folded together using the ring in the middle for saving space in the kitchen drawer. The ring also serves as a hanging fixture for easily accessible storage on the wall. Beater comes in white, grey, light blue, nude, lavender, pink, mint and navy blue. Each colour also has a contrast colour that gives the design life and humour. Use Beater to whisk eggs, sauce or delicious desserts. Suitable for dishwasher.
